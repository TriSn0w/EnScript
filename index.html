About EnScript

EnScript was started on May 8th, 2022, for the purpose of giving beginners to Node a easier experience, and for giving well-acquainted Node developers a different experience of Node, whilest keeping everything that Node has. The developer, Tristan (Gunner/TriSn0w) thought that he should make his own superset, because for him (at the beginning), Node was hard for him, so he wanted to make it easier for others, and himself simultaneously.
